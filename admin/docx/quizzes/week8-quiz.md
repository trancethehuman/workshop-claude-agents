# Kahoot: Week 8 - Demos & Retrospective

## Question 1: What is the recommended demo format for final presentations?

- ☐ 10 minutes presentation + 5 minutes Q&A
- ☒ 5 minutes presentation + 2 minutes Q&A
- ☐ 15 minutes presentation + 3 minutes Q&A
- ☐ 3 minutes presentation + 7 minutes Q&A

## Question 2: What should you lead with in your demo?

- ☐ The technology stack you used
- ☐ A detailed architecture diagram
- ☒ The business impact and pain point you solved
- ☐ A list of all the tools and skills you built

## Question 3: Why should you have a backup recording of your demo?

- ☐ It's required for grading purposes
- ☐ So other students can watch it later
- ☒ Live demos fail about 30% of the time
- ☐ Recording quality is always better than live

## Question 4: What is the correct structure for the demo presentation?

- ☐ Introduction → Architecture → Code Walkthrough → Q&A
- ☒ The Problem (30s) → The Solution (1min) → Live Demo (2.5min) → Learnings (1min)
- ☐ Background → Tools Used → Results → Future Plans
- ☐ Agenda → Technical Setup → Demo → Retrospective

## Question 5: Looking back at the full course, what was covered in Week 4?

- ☐ Setting up MCP servers for external services
- ☐ Building the Agent SDK in TypeScript
- ☒ Encoding domain expertise as reusable Skills (SKILL.md)
- ☐ Creating evaluation golden datasets

## Question 6: What did Vercel demonstrate about AI agents replacing human workers?

- ☐ AI agents can never replace human judgment
- ☒ They replaced 9 of 10 inbound SDRs with one person QA-ing an AI agent
- ☐ AI agents are only useful as assistants, not replacements
- ☐ They hired more people after implementing AI agents

## Question 7: Which peer voting category recognizes a project most likely to be used in a real business?

- ☐ Most Creative
- ☐ Best Demo
- ☒ Most Likely for Production
- ☐ Most Practical

## Question 8: What is the recommended feedback framework for peer demos?

- ☐ Score from 1-10 on technical complexity
- ☐ Thumbs up or thumbs down only
- ☒ What Worked Well → Questions → Suggestions
- ☐ Pros, Cons, and Overall Rating
