## Key Takeaways

- **Cursor** and **Replit** have the highest real-world valuations despite modest amounts in this database
- **Codeium/Windsurf** and **Codegen** were both acquired in 2025 — major consolidation in the space
- The market is splitting between **IDE-native tools** (Cursor, Continue, Windsurf) and **autonomous agents** (Codegen, Cosine, Aider)
